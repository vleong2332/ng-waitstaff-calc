Keep track of checks and earnings with this simple, little app that every waitstaff could appreciate.

Simply enter the price of the meal as listed on the menu, your restaurant's tax rate, and the percentage of the tip left by the customer, and "Waitstaff Calculator" will take care of the rest. This amazing app will remember your earnings so you can focus on doing your best.

This app is trully an exercise that implements basic Angular JS concepts such as modules, controllers, scope, templating, two-way data binding, form validation, routing, and views.
